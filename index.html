✬ What's a Meteor ?

-A meteor (aka a meteoroid or spacer rock) is a small body of matter from outer space that enters the earth atmosphere, becoming incandescent as a result of friction and appearing as streak of light.

✬ What's a meteor shower?

-A number of a number of meteors that appear to radiate from one point in the sky at a particular date each year, due to the earth's regularly passing through a field of particles at that position in its orbit.
